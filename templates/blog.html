Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever. With the increasing importance of remote healthcare solutions, we embarked on a journey to develop a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application. Leveraging Flask for the backend and a Decision Tree Classifier model, we created a user-friendly platform that can help users identify potential illnesses based on their reported symptoms.

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and seek quick answers about their health concerns. Differentiating between various diseases can be challenging, especially when symptoms overlap. Our goal was to provide a convenient solution for users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses.

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing. The app then uses a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model to predict the most likely disease based on the provided symptoms. Here's how it works:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through a user-friendly interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier predicts diseases based on input symptoms.
  3. Prediction: The app provides instant information about potential health concerns.
  4. Additional Information: The app offers disease descriptions, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: An intuitive design ensures easy symptom input.
  • Accurate Predictions: The ML model delivers reliable disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: The app includes disease descriptions, precautions, medications, diet recommendations, and workout tips.
  • Educational Content: Blog posts provide valuable health-related insights.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App brings the power of machine learning and healthcare information to users' fingertips. It serves as a valuable resource for individuals seeking quick and reliable health insights.
